Need to know

Location

There is parking available on-site, but spaces are limited, so visitors are encouraged to use public transport where possible.

Accessibility

We are committed to making our tours accessible to all visitors, with provisions in place for people with disabilities or mobility issues. If you have any specific accessibility requirements, please get in touch with The National Archives in advance to discuss your needs.
